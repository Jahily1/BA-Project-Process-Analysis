# Operational Efficiency & MIS Integration Analysis: Burrito Gringo Case Study

## Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive strategic analysis of **Burrito Gringo**, a Mexican-inspired fast-casual restaurant chain based in Ottawa. The focus is on driving **Digital Transformation** by integrating a Management Information System (MIS) to optimize operational efficiency and enhance customer experience.

## Business Context
Burrito Gringo is a competitive player in the local food service market, known for fresh ingredients and quick service. To maintain its leadership, the organization is shifting toward a tech-enabled service model that prioritizes:
* **Operational Efficiency:** Reducing food waste and manual monitoring.
* **Customer Convenience:** Expanding digital partnerships (Uber Eats, DoorDash).
* **Scalability:** Standardizing processes across multiple locations.

## Key Analytical Areas
* **Strategic Alignment:** Audited organizational goals and values to ensure technology adoption supports the brand's commitment to quality and authenticity.
* **Workflow Optimization:** Identified bottlenecks in current service delivery and inventory management.
* **MIS Integration:** Evaluated the implementation of tools like **Resto Soft** for inventory and digital platform synchronization.
* **Future State Modeling:** Explored advanced integrations including **IoT** for equipment monitoring, **AI** for marketing personalization, and **Blockchain** for supply chain transparency.

## Methodology & Deliverables
1. **Stakeholder Analysis:** Understanding the needs of both the operational teams and the end customers.
2. **Process Mapping:** * **As-Is Workflows:** Documenting the current state of manual inventory and delivery handling.
   * **To-Be Workflows:** Designing the future state post-MIS implementation to reduce "reprocess" and human error.
3. **Gap Analysis:** Bridging the divide between current capabilities and a tech-driven service model.
